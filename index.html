CHME 6390 — Professional Development & Communications Essentials

Adam Ekenseair

Associate Teaching Professor, Northeastern University

Prof. Ekenseair teaches this course.

Course Description

Explores topics related to building advanced careers in chemical engineering. Focuses on strategies for academic success, communication, and networking.

Offers students an opportunity to build a sense of cohort community and to learn about ongoing research projects in the Department of Chemical Engineering, professional workplace behaviors, and ethical conduct in a research environment.
